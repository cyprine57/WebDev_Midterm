A Beginner's Guide on Web Development

What is Web Development?

Web Development is a broad term that touches on the creation, development and maintaining of websites and web applications.

There are multiple sub-components that fall under web development and involves a wide range of tasks.


It is not only limited to the designing of website and web applications alone, but also the integration of back-end jobs such as in the context of database management.


I will be giving a beginner-friendly guide for the basic components in the creation of a website. From where its structure is created, styling websites/web applications and deploying them.
